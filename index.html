Invitation from our principal.

Vig English school, Chhota govindpur is organising the 2nd V-Genesis this year on 18th August 2018 to bring together young minds from all schools to demonstrate their technical and creative skills. The framework of the event is such that it will enrich student with new skills and novel way of thinking, learning, team-work, enterprenurship skill and leadership quality. It is a blend of activity revolving around themes like business, music, art, model-display, dance etc.

General Instructions

  1. Registration fee- ₹ 50
  2. Only students of classes IX , X , XI and XII can participate.
  3. A student may take part in only one event.
  4. Registration date-29th July, 2023. Name of the participants should be submitted on or before -4th August, 2023.
  5. Students should not disclose their schools name.
  6. Each participating school should be represented by a teacher in charge.
  7. School diary must be carried by all the students.
  8. In order to be eligible for the over-all championship, school must participate in the mentioned compulsory events.
  9. The dress code for the participants is white shirt and blue jeans / pants.
  10. Canteen facility will be available.
  11. Bonus points will be awarded to schools participating in all the events and for maintaining discipline.
  12. The judge's decision, in any event, will be FINAL.
  13. Participants may bring their own laptops and other required materials at their own risks, school will not be responsible for any lost items.
  14. Participants will not be allowed to use their phones during their events except the participants of CINEMATIC SERENITY.
  15. V-genesis will be a one day event.
