Invitation from our principal.

Vig English school, Chhota govindpur is organising the 2nd V-Genesis this year on 18th August 2018 to bring together young minds from all schools to demonstrate their technical and creative skills. The framework of the event is such that it will enrich student with new skills and novel way of thinking, learning, team-work, enterprenurship skill and leadership quality. It is a blend of activity revolving around themes like business, music, art, model-display, dance etc.

General Rules

  1. All School are requested to send the names of the participants
    on or before August 11,2018 through email/hard copy.
  2. Registration time 7:30 am to 8:30 am.
  3. All the information regarding the events will be provided
    at the registration table.
  4. Participants must take care of their own belongings.
    (VES will not be responsible under any circumstances)
  5. Each school team should be escorted by a teacher.The
    teacher escort will be responsible for the discipline
    of their own students and their belongings.
  6. All participants are required to get themselves registered
    otherwise they will not be enlisted.
  7. Every team members must carry their valid ID card issued by
    their school.
  8. Participants must be from IX to XII.
  9. Dress code will be white shirt/t-shirt and black/blue jeans.
  10. Entry fees per participant is Rs. 50 /- (Rs. Fifty only).
  11. Eatables will be on sale at the school canteen.
